🔗 Note Linking

[[Note Title]]Note Title
[[Title|alias]]alias

Use the link toolbar button or type [[ to link between notes. Notes that are linked to automatically show a Linked from backlinks panel at the bottom of their view.

🏷️ Tags

Type in the tag bar below the title. Press Enter or , to add. Tags are colour-coded and appear in the sidebar — click any tag to filter the note list to just that tag.

⚡ Slash Commands

Type / at the start of a line or after a space to open the command palette. Use ↑↓ to navigate, Enter to insert, Esc to dismiss.

/h1Heading 1
/h2Heading 2
/todoTask list item
/table2-column table
/linkNote link picker

🕸️ Note Graph

Press G anywhere or click the graph icon in the sidebar to open a live force-directed map of all your notes and their [[links]].

Drag nodeReposition note
Click nodeOpen note & close graph
ScrollZoom in / out
Drag canvasPan view

Larger nodes have more incoming links. The currently open note is highlighted in purple.
